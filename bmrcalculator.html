This means that your body needs approximately 1,600 calories per day to maintain basic functions. If you consume this amount, you will neither gain nor lose weight unless additional physical activity is considered.

Personalized Recommendations Based on Your BMR

Tailored guidance to help you achieve your health and fitness goals by adjusting your caloric intake based on your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR).

Weight Maintenance Recommendation

To maintain your current weight, aim to consume approximately 2,400 calories per day based on your activity level.

Tip: Keep your calorie intake consistent with your activity level and incorporate meals with a variety of nutrients to sustain your energy throughout the day.

Weight Loss Recommendation

To lose weight, aim for a daily intake of 1,700-1,900 calories/day. This caloric deficit will help you lose approximately 1-1.5 lbs per week.

Tip: Incorporate regular physical activity such as cardio, strength training, and flexibility exercises to enhance fat loss while maintaining muscle mass.
